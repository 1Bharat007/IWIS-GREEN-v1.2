# Sustainability and ESG

## Sustainable Development
Development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It requires balancing economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental protection.

## Circular Economy
A systemic approach to economic development designed to benefit businesses, society, and the environment. In contrast to the 'take-make-dispose' linear model, a circular economy is regenerative by design and aims to gradually decouple growth from the consumption of finite resources.
**Principles:**
1. Eliminate waste and pollution.
2. Circulate products and materials (at their highest value).
3. Regenerate nature.

## ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance)
A framework designed to be embedded into an organization's strategy that considers needs and ways to generate value for all organizational stakeholders.
*   **Environmental:** How a company safeguards the environment (e.g., corporate policies addressing climate change, waste management).
*   **Social:** How a company manages relationships with employees, suppliers, customers, and communities.
*   **Governance:** Deals with a company’s leadership, executive pay, audits, internal controls, and shareholder rights.

## SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals)
A collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all" established by the United Nations. Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and Goal 13 (Climate Action) are highly relevant to waste management.

## Net Zero and Carbon Footprint
*   **Carbon Footprint:** The total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions.
*   **Net Zero:** Achieving a state in which the greenhouse gases going into the atmosphere are balanced by removal out of the atmosphere.

## Sustainable Lifestyles
Adopting habits that minimize environmental impact. Examples include zero-waste shopping, using public transport, reducing meat consumption, and active recycling.
