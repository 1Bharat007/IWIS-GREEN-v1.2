# IWIS Platform Knowledge

## What is IWIS?
IWIS (Integrated Waste Intelligence System) is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize waste management by connecting citizens, municipalities, and recyclers.

## AI Waste Scanner
The core feature of the IWIS app. Users take a photo of an item, and the AI instantly classifies it (Plastic, Metal, Organic, etc.), explains how to dispose of it, and calculates the CO2 emissions saved by recycling it.

## EcoBot
The intelligent AI assistant built into IWIS. It can answer questions about waste segregation, recycling processes, climate change, and how to use the IWIS app.

## Green Points and Rewards
Every time a user successfully scans and segregates waste, they earn Green Points. These points contribute to their rank on the Global Leaderboard. Future updates will allow points to be redeemed for eco-friendly products or local municipal tax rebates.

## Hotspot Reporting
Citizens can use the app to photograph and report illegal garbage dumps (hotspots). The app captures the GPS coordinates and sends an alert directly to the municipal dashboard for cleanup.

## Circular Exchange (Marketplace)
A B2B and C2B marketplace where bulk generators of waste (e.g., a society with 50kg of newspaper, or an office with e-waste) can list their material. Certified recyclers bid on these listings and arrange pickups, creating a transparent, localized circular economy.

## Municipal Dashboard
A web portal for city officials to view real-time analytics, track garbage truck routes, monitor reported hotspots, and analyze the city's overall recycling rates and CO2 diversion metrics.

## Food Rescue Network
A feature connecting bulk food generators (weddings, restaurants) with verified NGOs to instantly redistribute surplus safe food to marginalized communities, preventing it from reaching landfills.
